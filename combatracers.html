Key Responsiblities: Gamepad Inputs, Gameplay, Spotlights Behaviour

My responsiblities involved integrating gamepad controls with customizability options as well as spotlight rigging to focus on players. Attended Games First with the producer to get feedback from Supercell employees and other Finnish game developers.

Ashes: Rise of the Phoenixborn

Key Responsiblities: Playtesting Feedback, Proofreading


Back in 2017, I was serving my National Service so I had time on my hands. Ashes was one of the card games I was playing and saw they were accepting playtesters. Applied, got accepted and the rest is history.

Responsiblities involved testing several expansions and seeing how it would impact the game. Proofread rules to make sure they were unambiguous and did not conflict with previous cards/rules. When the game was rebooted into Ashes: Reborn, I was part of the team that streamlined the rules and card text.

Check the game out at the Plaidhat Store or play it online on Ashteki.
